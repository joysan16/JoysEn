FAQ

Common questions

Is there a cost to join as a startup?

Pre-Incubation is completely free. The Incubation Program is equity-based (typically 2-5%), while Acceleration uses a performance-linked fee. Our Social Innovation track is grant-based with no equity required.

How long does the application process take?

You'll typically hear back within 48 hours of submitting your application, followed by a 20-minute screening call within the week if shortlisted.

Can I apply if I don't have a registered company yet?

Yes — our Pre-Incubation stage is designed exactly for this. You can apply with just an idea; we'll help you validate it before you need to register anything.

How does mentor matching work?

After your screening call, our team matches you with a mentor based on your sector, stage, and specific challenge — not randomly assigned. See the Mentors page for more.

Do you support startups outside major cities?

Yes — bridging this gap is one of our core objectives. We have member incubators and remote mentoring support across 15 states, including Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.

How can my incubator or institution join the network?

Use the Partnership Inquiry form on this page and select "Academic Partnership" or "Government Collaboration" — our partnerships team will reach out to discuss next steps.
