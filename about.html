About JoyEn Group

Fueling India's next generation of entrepreneurs

JoyEn Group Incubator Association is a not-for-profit body connecting universities, corporates, government, and investors to create India's most inclusive startup ecosystem.

Founded in 2018, we've grown to 48 member incubators across 15 states.

Our Vision

India as the world's most vibrant startup nation — where every entrepreneur, regardless of geography, gender, or background, gets a genuine shot at building something meaningful.

Our Mission

To connect, support, and scale startups through a unified national incubation network — bringing universities, corporates, and government schemes under one accessible roof.

Our Values

Innovation over convention. Inclusive access to opportunity. Radical transparency. Long-term thinking over quick wins. Community before competition.

What Drives Us

Our core objectives

Four commitments that guide every program, partnership, and decision we make.

01

Bridge the Opportunity Gap

Ensure every startup — regardless of geography or background — gets equal access to mentorship, funding, and infrastructure, not just those in metro cities.

02

Foster Cross-Sector Collaboration

Connect academia, industry, and government bodies so resources and knowledge flow freely instead of staying siloed inside individual institutions.

03

Drive Measurable Impact

Track real outcomes — jobs created, funding raised, and lives improved — and publish annual transparency reports for our member institutions and partners.

04

Build for Longevity

Design programs and partnerships meant to outlast any single cohort, creating durable infrastructure for India's innovation ecosystem for decades to come.

Our Journey

From a small campus initiative to a national network

2018

Founded at IIT Madras Research Park

Started as a small alumni-led initiative connecting 5 founding incubators across South India.

2019 — 2020

National Expansion Begins

Grew to 18 member incubators across 8 states; launched our first formal Pre-Incubation Program.

2021

First Unicorn-Track Startup

AgroMind AI, incubated through our network, raised its first institutional round — validating the model at scale.

2022 — 2023

Government Recognition

Registered as a Section 8 company; recognized by DPIIT as an official Startup India ecosystem enabler.

2024

120+ Mentor Network Launched

Formalized our mentor program with structured matching, onboarding 120+ industry experts across 12 domains.

2025

48 Incubators, 340+ Startups

Today we operate India's largest cross-institutional incubator network, spanning 15 states and ₹280 Cr+ in cumulative funding raised by our startups.
