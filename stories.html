AgroMind AI

Started in our pre-incubation cohort in 2020, AgroMind AI built a precision agriculture platform using satellite imagery and soil sensors to help smallholder farmers increase yields by up to 30%. The founders — two agricultural engineering graduates — had no prior startup experience.

Through JoyEn's incubation program, they received ₹15L in seed funding, were matched with an AgriTech-focused mentor, and got introduced to their first institutional investor at our 2021 Demo Day. Today the company serves 50,000 farmers across 8 states and recently closed a Series B round.

🚀 Fastest Growth

MedQuick Health

MedQuick entered our Acceleration program in 2022 with just 3 founders and a basic telemedicine prototype. They needed help navigating healthcare regulations and scaling their engineering team fast.

Our mentor network connected them with a healthcare compliance expert and a former Google product VP. Within 24 months, MedQuick grew from 3 founders to 400 employees, expanded into 5 countries, and now generates ₹120 Cr in annual recurring revenue.

💚 Social Impact

EduBridge

EduBridge joined our Social Innovation track in 2021 with a mission: bring quality education in regional languages to rural India. With zero VC funding and a grant-based model, they relied entirely on JoyEn's CSR funding connections and government scheme navigation support.

Today, EduBridge has reached 2 million learners across 18 Indian languages, partnering with state education departments in 6 states — proving that impact-first startups can scale without traditional venture funding.
