About JoyEn Group

Fueling India's next generation of entrepreneurs

JoyEn Group Incubator Association is a not-for-profit body connecting universities, corporates, government, and investors to create India's most inclusive startup ecosystem.

Founded in 2018, we've grown to 48 member incubators across 15 states.

Bridge the Opportunity Gap

Every startup — regardless of geography — gets equal access to resources.

Foster Collaboration

Connecting academia, industry, and government for coordinated growth.

Drive Measurable Impact

Tracking jobs created, funds raised, and lives improved by our startups.

Our Vision

India as the world's most vibrant startup nation — where every idea gets its shot.

Our Mission

Connect, support, and scale startups through a unified national incubation network.

Our Values

Innovation · Inclusion · Transparency · Long-term Thinking · Community First
