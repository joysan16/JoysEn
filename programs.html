Program Details

Choose the right track for your stage

Stage 01 · 3 Months

Pre-Incubation

For founders with just an idea. Validate your concept, build your first business model, and get matched with an early-stage mentor.

Eligibility: Open to students, first-time founders, and anyone with an early-stage idea. No prior registration needed.

Cost: Completely free.

Stage 02 · 12 Months

Incubation Program

Build your MVP with structured mentorship, legal/IP support, and access to seed funding up to ₹25 lakhs.

Eligibility: Registered entity (or willing to register), working prototype or clear technical plan.

Cost: Equity-based, typically 2-5% depending on funding received.

Stage 03 · 6 Months

Acceleration

For startups with traction, ready to scale. Get Series A readiness support, CXO mentoring, and corporate partnership access.

Eligibility: Minimum ₹10L+ MRR or equivalent traction metrics, existing customer base.

Cost: Performance-linked fee structure.

Special Track · Flexible

Social Innovation

Dedicated support for impact-driven startups in healthcare, education, and sustainability with grant-based funding access.

Eligibility: Demonstrable social impact thesis, willingness to track impact metrics.

Cost: Grant-based, no equity required.
